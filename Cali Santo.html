Cali Santo

Hastings born, Brighton based Cali Santo is a surging songwriting talent with a deftness for fusing heartfelt lyricism with dream-like atmospheres. Readying his heavily layered and beautifully unfolding debut for QMN, Cali Santo brings his acoustic roots up to date with staggering dexterity.
