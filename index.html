22nd september 2017

Quietly Making Noise announces it's commitment to intelligent electronic music with a new EP from Brighton based producer sdxn. After establishing his arrival as a genuine production talent in 2015 with the acclaimed Only Water EP, sdxn returns with a 5 track offering of minimal electronics aimed at both open-minded dance floors and after hours stargazing.
